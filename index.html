About the Laboratory

The Solid Mechanics and Noise, Vibration & Harshness (NVH) Laboratory at IIT Indore is dedicated to cutting-edge research in structural dynamics, condition monitoring, machine learning, signal processing and predictive maintenance. The laboratory focuses on developing intelligent diagnostic and prognostic systems for modern engineering applications.

Principal Investigator

Prof. Anand Parey

Professor & Head
Department of Mechanical Engineering
Indian Institute of Technology Indore


Research Interests: Structural Dynamics, Rotating Machinery, Fault Diagnosis, Vibration Analysis, Condition Monitoring and Predictive Maintenance.
